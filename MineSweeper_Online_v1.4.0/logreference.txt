********************************
MineSweeper Online Log Reference
********************************

Classic Mode:
-------------

-   Non-clicked square
*   Mine
!   Clicked mine (game over)
?   Question mark
X   Wrongly flagged square (no mine)
F   Flagged mine
0-8 Number of mines in adjecent squares


Hotseat/Online Modes:
---------------------

-   Non-clicked square
*   Mine
R   Flagged by Red
B   Flagged by Blue
Y   Flagged by Yellow
P   Flagged by Purple
0-8 Number of mines in adjacent squares
?   ? square
